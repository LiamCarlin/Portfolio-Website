DPA Poker: Redefining Social Poker

Overview

DPA Poker is the ultimate tool for poker enthusiasts who value the social, face-to-face experience of in-person games. By combining cutting-edge technology with a focus on usability, DPA Poker transforms the way games are managed, tracked, and enjoyed. It’s not just an app; it’s the perfect companion for your next poker night.

The Challenge: Keeping Poker Fun and Fair

Managing poker games can be tedious. Players struggle with manual chip counting, payout calculations, and remembering game stats. The fun often gets overshadowed by the work of managing the game. Online poker apps don’t solve this problem—they remove the human connection, emphasizing gambling over genuine social interaction.

Our Solution: A Smarter Poker Companion

DPA Poker brings the best of both worlds by leveraging technology to enhance, not replace, the social aspect of poker. Its features are designed to eliminate manual effort while making gameplay more exciting and transparent.

Key Features

Chip Counting with Machine Learning

  • Use your phone's camera to scan and count poker chips in seconds
  • Tracks chip values for every player and updates the game in real-time

Integrated Bank and Cash Payouts

  • Acts as a virtual bank for your poker game, logging each player's buy-ins and cash-outs
  • Automatically calculates payouts at the end of the game, ensuring every player receives the right amount

Hand Probability Insights

  • Leverages ML to calculate the odds of winning based on your hand and the community cards
  • Provides real-time decision-making assistance without disrupting the flow of the game

Detailed Game Stats and Leaderboards

  • Tracks group and individual statistics over multiple games
  • Generates reports and leaderboards to showcase performance, adding a friendly competitive edge

Customizable Rooms

  • Create private rooms for your games
  • Invite friends to join and play, track stats, and even save the history of past games

How It Works

  • Set Up Your Game: Create a room, invite your friends, and start playing. DPA Poker keeps everything organized.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Use the chip counter and hand probability tools during the game to streamline gameplay and make smarter decisions.
  • End-Game Payouts: Once the game is over, the app calculates and divides the winnings for you—no confusion, no mistakes.
  • Review Your Progress: Dive into detailed stats for yourself or your group. See who's winning big, who's improving, and who owes bragging rights.

Technology Behind DPA Poker

Front-End

Built in React Native, ensuring a seamless, user-friendly experience across iOS and Android devices.

Back-End

Features a blend of JavaScript for business logic and Python for camera-related machine learning tasks.

Machine Learning

  • Chip counting: Uses object detection and image classification
  • Hand probabilities: Employs advanced ML algorithms
